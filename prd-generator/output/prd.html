Section 02

Executive Summary

BusinessNow PSA is the operational system of record for KSAP Technology's professional services business. It replaces a fragmented landscape of spreadsheets, email-based approvals, and disconnected point-tools with a single, role-aware platform that covers the full lifecycle from sales prospect through cash collection.

The Opportunity

KSAP currently loses an estimated 4–6% of annual revenue to billing leakage caused by untracked time, late invoices, and scope-change drift. Resource utilisation sits at 64% against a target of 75%, primarily because PMs cannot see capacity vs demand without manually consolidating data from three systems. Project health degradation is typically detected 2–3 weeks late, after budget overrun has occurred.

The Solution

A unified PSA built on modern web foundations (React, Express, PostgreSQL) with seven integrated modules: CRM, Project Delivery, Resource Management, Time & Attendance, Finance, Reports, and Admin. Cross-module automation (auto-allocation on opportunity probability, auto-invoice on milestone completion, AI-ranked resource suggestions) eliminates the manual handoffs that produce errors and delay.

Product Vision & Goals

Vision Statement

"BusinessNow PSA is the operational backbone for KSAP Technology's professional services business — unifying CRM, project delivery, resource planning, time tracking, and finance into a single source of truth that eliminates spreadsheets, prevents revenue leakage, and gives leaders real-time visibility from sales pipeline through cash collection."

Strategic Goals

IDGoalSuccess MetricTarget
G1 Eliminate manual handoffs between sales and delivery 100% of won opportunities convert to projects in < 1 click Q2 2026
G2 Achieve > 75% organisation-wide billable utilisation Billable hours / total available hours per consultant Q3 2026
G3 Reduce timesheet approval cycle from 5 days to 24 hours Median time from submit → approve Q2 2026
G4 Auto-draft invoices on milestone completion % of invoices created without manual data entry Q2 2026
G5 Surface project health degradation within 48 hours % of At Risk / Off Track projects flagged on dashboard Q3 2026
G6 Capacity-plan 12 weeks ahead with < 5% forecast error Planned hours vs actual hours, rolling 12-week Q4 2026

Non-Goals

The following are explicitly out of scope for this product:

  • Multi-tenant SaaS — this is a single-tenant deployment for KSAP only.
  • General-ledger accounting — we hand off to NetSuite for GL, AR, AP.
  • Payroll calculation — we provide approved timesheet exports only.
  • Customer-facing project portal beyond read-only status views.
  • Mobile-native apps — responsive web is in scope; native iOS/Android is not.

Modules Overview

BusinessNow PSA is composed of seven integrated modules accessible via a single navigation. Each module is independently deployable but shares a common data model, authentication, and audit infrastructure.

Manage the full sales funnel — from first-touch prospects through to won opportunities ready for delivery.

Section 08

Workflows & Process Flows

The four workflows below describe the cross-module business processes that BusinessNow PSA automates. Each diagram shows the actor responsible for each step, colour-coded by role.

Sales → Delivery (Opportunity to Project)

How a qualified opportunity becomes a delivered project, with resourcing reserved early.
#ActorActionDetail
1 Sales Create Opportunity Account, value, expected close, stage
2 Sales Update probability When ≥ 70%, system auto-creates a Soft Allocation
3 PM Review soft allocation Confirm proposed consultants on Resources page
4 Sales Mark opportunity Won Triggers conversion eligibility
5 PM Convert → Project POST /api/opportunities/:id/convert with template
6 System Hard-allocate resources Soft allocations promoted to Hard
7 PM Kick off delivery Phase plan, task assignment, baseline snapshot

Time → Approval → Invoice

From a logged hour through approval into a draft invoice.
#ActorActionDetail
1 Consultant Open weekly timesheet Import allocations to pre-fill rows
2 Consultant Log hours daily Optional: AI Time Assistant suggests entries
3 Consultant Submit for approval POST /api/timesheets/:id/submit
4 PM Review queue /time?tab=approvals shows pending
5 PM Approve or Reject Reject requires a comment
6 System Lock approved entries Audit-locked; no further edits
7 System Roll into invoice Time-and-materials invoices include approved hours × bill rate

Resource Request → Allocation

How unmet demand on a project is fulfilled.
#ActorActionDetail
1 PM Identify gap Capacity Planning shows red over-allocation or empty role
2 PM Raise request Type: New / Replace / Extend / Reduce / Change Role / Remove
3 Resource Manager Triage request Open queue grouped by project / urgency
4 Resource Manager Get AI suggestions POST /api/resources/suggest — top 3 by composite score
5 Resource Manager Confirm allocation Hard allocation created; consultant notified
6 PM Acknowledge Request status → Fulfilled

Milestone → Auto-Draft Invoice

Milestone completion creates a billable event.
#ActorActionDetail
1 Consultant Complete milestone task Mark task with isMilestone = true as Done
2 System Detect milestone Trigger fires on status change
3 System Create draft invoice POST /api/invoices with line items from milestone value
4 Finance Review draft Edit line items, attach PO if needed
5 Finance Send invoice Status: Draft → Sent; due date set
6 Finance Mark Paid On payment receipt; Revenue Entry recognised per policy

Section 12

Acceptance Criteria

The following criteria define "done" for v1.0 of BusinessNow PSA. All must be met before sign-off.

  1. All 7 modules accessible from the sidebar with role-appropriate visibility
  2. Server-side RBAC enforced on every write endpoint (verified by automated tests)
  3. Audit log entry created on every POST/PATCH/DELETE
  4. Onboarding checklist auto-dismisses when all 4 conditions are met
  5. Capacity Planning report renders within 2 seconds for 52-week × 50-person dataset
  6. Timesheet import and submit work end-to-end without page refresh
  7. Opportunity conversion creates project, allocations, and audit entry atomically
  8. Auto-draft invoice fires within 5 seconds of milestone completion
  9. Document Templates 'From Template' button populates fields correctly
  10. All forms have proper validation and error messages
Section 13

Risks & Assumptions

IDRiskProbabilityImpactMitigation
R1 Single-tenant scope creep into multi-tenant Medium High Architecture review gate on any tenant-scoping PR; product steering reaffirms single-tenant.
R2 Auto-invoice misfires on accidentally-completed milestones Medium Medium Drafts are always editable; PMs receive notification before invoice is sent.
R3 AI resource suggestions perceived as biased / opaque Low Medium Always show 'reasons' array; allow PMs to override; log overrides for analysis.
R4 Audit log volume degrades query performance Medium Medium Partition by month; archive > 18 months to cold storage.
R5 Timesheet rejection comments not actioned Low Low Notification to consultant on reject; SLA dashboard for unresolved rejections.
