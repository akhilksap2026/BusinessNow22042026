**Subject:** Quick look — BusinessNow PSA, what it does

Hi Dan,

Wanted to give you a quick walk-through of BusinessNow PSA — the platform we've built to run our professional services business end-to-end. Here's what it does, module by module:

**CRM** — Tracks accounts, prospects, and opportunities. Once a deal hits 70% probability, the system automatically reserves consultants for it, so we're not scrambling to staff things at the last minute. One click converts a won deal into a live project.

**Project Delivery** — The heart of the platform. Plan phases and tasks, set baselines, assign owners, and watch project health (On Track / At Risk / Off Track) in real time. Project templates mean we stop rebuilding the same plan from scratch.

**Resource Management** — Shows who is busy, who is free, and where we have skill gaps — up to 12 weeks ahead. When a PM needs a person, the system suggests the top 3 candidates based on skills and availability, with a clear reason for each suggestion.

**Time & Attendance** — Weekly timesheets that pre-fill from your active allocations, an AI assistant that suggests time entries from your calendar, and a one-click submit/approve flow. Time-off requests live here too.

**Finance** — Maintains rate cards, raises invoices, and recognises revenue. The biggest win: when a milestone is marked complete, a draft invoice is created automatically so we stop forgetting to bill.

**Reports & Analytics** — 10+ pre-built reports covering utilisation, capacity, project health, revenue, and pipeline forecast. Exportable to CSV or Excel.

**Admin** — User management, role-based access (Admin / Super User / Consultant / Customer), document templates, and a full audit trail of every change made in the system.

**The thread that ties it together:** sales hands off cleanly to delivery, delivery automatically pulls the right resources, time gets logged against real allocations, milestones trigger billing, and leadership sees portfolio health without chasing spreadsheets.
