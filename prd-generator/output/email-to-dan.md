**Subject:** Quick look — BusinessNow PSA, what it does

Hi Dan,

Wanted to give you a quick walk-through of BusinessNow PSA — the platform we've built to run our professional services business end-to-end. Here's what each module does:

**CRM — Sales Funnel**
- Manage accounts, prospects, and opportunities in one place
- Track pipeline stage, value, and probability per deal
- Auto-reserves consultants when a deal hits 70% probability (no last-minute scrambling)
- One-click conversion of a won deal into a live project with team and dates pre-filled
- Roll-up of pipeline value per account for relationship prioritisation

**Project Delivery — The Heart of the Platform**
- Build project plans with phases, tasks, dependencies, and milestones
- Health indicator on every project: On Track / At Risk / Off Track
- Project templates so we stop rebuilding the same plan from scratch
- Baselines to snapshot the plan and track variance over time
- Change orders with formal approval flow
- Bulk task updates (status, owner, priority) to rebalance a sprint quickly

**Resource Management — Right Person, Right Time**
- Visual capacity heatmap showing who is busy or free, up to 12 weeks ahead
- Hard vs soft allocations (committed vs probable demand)
- Skills matrix with proficiency tracking per consultant
- AI-ranked suggestions for staffing — top 3 candidates with a clear "why" for each
- Resource request workflow (new, replace, extend, reduce, change role, remove)
- Time-off and public holidays factored into capacity automatically

**Time & Attendance — Effortless Timesheets**
- Weekly timesheet grid that pre-fills from your active project allocations
- AI Time Assistant suggests entries based on calendar and activity
- One-click submit, with PM approval queue for review and reject (with comments)
- Approved entries are locked to prevent retroactive edits
- Time-off requests and balance tracking, including team approval

**Finance — Stop Leaving Money on the Table**
- Rate cards (bill rate and cost rate) per role per project, with effective dates
- Auto-drafted invoices the moment a milestone is marked complete
- Billing schedules — recurring or milestone-based, set once and forget
- Revenue recognition tracked separately from invoicing (recognition vs cash)
- Clean export hand-off to NetSuite for the GL

**Reports & Analytics — Visibility for Leaders**
- Capacity Planning (52-week supply vs demand)
- Utilisation by consultant, department, or region
- Project Health snapshot across the entire portfolio
- Pipeline forecast (weighted by probability)
- Budget vs Actual, Revenue Recognition, Time-off Liability, Skills Coverage gaps
- Export to CSV, Excel, or PDF

**Admin — Control & Governance**
- User management with 4 clear access levels (Admin, PM/Finance, Consultant, Customer)
- Role-based access enforced everywhere — people only see what they should
- Project and document templates so teams reuse approved content
- Audit log capturing every change in the system, with who and when
- System-wide settings: regions, holidays, branding, custom fields

**The thread that ties it together:** sales hands off cleanly to delivery, delivery automatically pulls the right resources, time gets logged against real allocations, milestones trigger billing, and leadership sees portfolio health without chasing spreadsheets.
