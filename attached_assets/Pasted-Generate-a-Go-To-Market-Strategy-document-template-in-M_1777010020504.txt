Generate a Go-To-Market Strategy document template in Markdown format for a SaaS product launch.
Save it to /docs/business/07_go_to_market_strategy.md

Use [BRACKETS] for all placeholders. This document is for the business, marketing, and sales teams.

Sections:

1. Document Header
   - Product: [PRODUCT NAME], Target Launch: [QUARTER + YEAR], Owner: [CMO/GM Name]

2. Executive Summary
   - Product elevator pitch (2-3 sentences)
   - Target market overview
   - Launch goal: [X users / $X ARR in first 6 months]

3. Target Market
   - TAM / SAM / SOM estimates (use $[X]M placeholders)
   - Primary segment: [Industry, company size, geography]
   - Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) description
   - Buyer vs. User distinction

4. Value Proposition
   - Core value prop statement
   - Benefit table: Benefit | Customer Pain It Solves | Competitors Who Don't Solve This

5. Pricing Strategy
   - Pricing model: [Freemium / Per Seat / Usage-based / Flat - TBD]
   - Pricing tiers table: Tier | Price/mo | Features Included | Target Segment
   - Free trial details: [X day trial / freemium]

6. Competitive Landscape
   - Competitor table: Competitor | Strengths | Weaknesses | Our Advantage
   - Positioning statement: "For [target user] who [need], [Product] is the [category] that [key benefit], unlike [competitor] which [their weakness]."

7. Distribution Channels
   - Primary: [Direct / PLG / Sales-led / Partnership - TBD]
   - Channel table: Channel | Effort | Expected Reach | Owner | Timeline

8. Launch Phases
   - Phase 1 — Private Beta: Goals, Duration, Target participants
   - Phase 2 — Public Beta: Goals, Duration, Success criteria
   - Phase 3 — GA Launch: Goals, Date, KPIs

9. Marketing Plan
   - Pre-launch activities (checklist)
   - Launch day activities (checklist)
   - Post-launch activities (checklist)
   - Content calendar outline (first 3 months)
   - Budget allocation table: Channel | Budget | Expected Output

10. Sales Strategy
    - Sales motion: [Inbound / Outbound / PLG-led - TBD]
    - Sales cycle length estimate
    - Demo/trial conversion target
    - Key sales tools and collateral needed

11. Success Metrics
    - Table: Metric | Target | Timeframe | Owner

12. Risks & Mitigations
    - Table: Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation

Format in clean Markdown with tables and bullet points.
