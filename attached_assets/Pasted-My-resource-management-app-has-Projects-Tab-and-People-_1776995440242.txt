My resource management app has Projects Tab and People Tab 
with a Gantt-style timeline of allocation bars. 
Allocation bars are already rendered per team member per project.

TASK: Add three UI features. Do not refactor existing components.

--- FEATURE 1: KPI Summary Bar ---
Add a sticky bar at the top of the Resource Management module 
(above both tabs). Show 4 live metrics computed from current data:
- Total Capacity: sum of all team members' (daily_capacity × 
  working_days in current month)
- Total Allocated Hours: sum of all hard+soft allocation hours 
  in current month
- Utilisation %: (Total Allocated / Total Capacity) × 100, 
  shown as a percentage with a colour: green <80%, amber 80–99%, 
  red ≥100%
- Over-Allocated: count of team members where their allocated 
  hours/day exceed daily_capacity on ANY day this month — 
  show as "X members" in red if > 0

Metrics should recalculate when the user changes the date range 
filter (if one exists), otherwise default to current month.

--- FEATURE 2: Over-Allocation Indicator on Timeline Bars ---
For each allocation bar already rendered on the timeline:
- Query: for each day in the bar's range, sum ALL allocation 
  hours for that team member across ALL projects
- If the daily sum exceeds member.daily_capacity on any day 
  within this bar's range: render a red line/stripe at the 
  TOP of that bar (do not replace the bar colour, overlay it)
- The red indicator should be visible in both Projects Tab 
  and People Tab

--- FEATURE 3: Bandwidth Tooltip on Hover ---
When a user hovers over any team member's row (in People Tab) 
or any allocation bar (in Projects Tab), show a tooltip with:
- Total Capacity: member.daily_capacity × days in view period
- Hard Allocations: sum of hard allocation hours in period
- Soft Allocations: sum of soft allocation hours in period  
- Remaining: Total Capacity − Hard − Soft (show in red if negative)
Tooltip should respect the current timeline granularity 
(day / week / month).

Use the existing design system / component library already 
in the project. Do not introduce new UI libraries.
