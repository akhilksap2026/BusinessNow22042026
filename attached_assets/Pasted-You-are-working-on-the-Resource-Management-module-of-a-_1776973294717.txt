STEP 2 — IMPLEMENT CHANGES:

2a. ROLE FIELD:
- Convert the Role field in team allocation from free-text to a

    - Role (from the controlled dropdown defined above)

    - Required role
    - Required skills (multi-select from the same skill list)
    - Required competency level per skill
    - Date range and hours per week
- On submitting the request, the system runs a matching algorithm:
    - Score each employee by: (matched skills at required competency 
      level or above / total required skills) × 100
    - Filter to show only candidates scoring 50% or above.
    - Sort candidates by score descending.
    - Display score as "XX% Match" badge next to each candidate name.

- Within the candidate selection view, display a utilization heat map 
  per candidate showing week-by-week availability for the requested 
  date range.
- Color coding: Green (available), Amber (partially allocated), 
  Red (fully booked).
- The heat map should appear inline below each candidate card, 
  expandable on click.

- Remove any years-of-experience field from employee profiles entirely.
- Remove any unstructured skill text fields — replace with the
