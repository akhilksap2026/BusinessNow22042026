ITEM 6 OF 6 — Resource AI — Allocation Suggestions
────────────────────────────────────────────────
When a PM is adding an allocation on a project, offer AI-powered suggestions for which team member to assign based on skills, availability, and role fit.

Backend tasks (new file: artifacts/api-server/src/routes/resourceSuggestions.ts):
1. Add endpoint: POST /resources/suggest — accepts { projectId: number, roleRequired?: string, skillIds?: number[], startDate: string, endDate: string, hoursPerWeek?: number }. Logic:
   a. Query all internal users with their skills (from skills_matrix or users + skills tables).
   b. Query existing allocations to compute each user's booked hours in the requested date range.
